== Description ==

Shift is a simple and customizable theme made for businesses and bloggers. Present your best content in a beautiful and responsive design across mobile devices, tablets, and widescreen monitors with Shift. With a minimalist design, Shift is great at highlighting your content. That means you’ll get higher engagement whether you’re publishing videos, podcast, photographs, or blog posts. It’s suitable for niche niche from travel and food blogs to corporate business websites. Use Shift for a small, personal content site or publish a ton with an online magazine.

**Live Demo**

View a fully-setup live demo of Shift here: [Live demo](https://www.competethemes.com/demos/?theme=shift)

**Features**

Shift comes with the following features:

* Custom logo
* Social media icons
* Show full posts or excerpts
* Custom excerpt length
* Custom Read More button text
* Make first post on blog extra wide
* Hide/Show display controls

**Shift Pro**

Shift also has an upgrade called [Shift Pro](https://www.competethemes.com/shift-pro/) that comes with the following features:

* Custom colors
* Featured sliders
* Featured videos
* Additional layouts
* Flexible header image
* Google fonts
* More widget areas
* Fixed Menu
* Background Images & Textures
* Display controls
* Custom footer text
